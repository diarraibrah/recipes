Ingredients:
- 1 1/2 cups cooked rice
- 1 1/2 cups milk
- 1 egg
- 3 teaspoons sugar
- 1/8 teaspoon salt
- 1/5 cup raisins
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1/4 cup butter

Directions:
1. In a clean saucepan, combine 1 1/2 cups cooked rice, 1 cup milk, sugar and salt.
2. Cook over medium heat until thick and creamy, 15 to 20 minutes
3. Stir in remaining 1/2 cup milk, beaten egg, and raisins
4. cook 2 minutes more, stirring constantly. 
5. Remove from heat and stir in butter and vanilla.
